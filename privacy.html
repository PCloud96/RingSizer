Privacy Policy

Ring Sizer · Cloud Motion Lab LLC · Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Ring Sizer is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data you enter stays on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No account or registration required
  • No personal information collected
  • No location data
  • No microphone or camera access
  • No data sent to our servers

Data Stored on Your Device

Ring Sizer stores your saved ring measurements and app settings locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Advertising

The free version of Ring Sizer displays ads served by Unity Ads. Unity Ads may collect limited device information (such as device type and IP address) to serve contextual ads. Unity Ads does not require App Tracking Transparency permission. For Unity's privacy policy, visit unity.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Purchases

Ring Sizer offers an optional one-time Pro upgrade to remove ads and unlock all features. The purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

Ring Sizer is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
